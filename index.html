Kathmandu

Uttar Pradesh has been the heart of Indian politics for over 5 centuries. Famous for Taj Mahal, Kashi pilgrimage, leather goods, refineries etc.

-4 Days-

Day Place Activity
1 ARRIVE AT KATHMANDU Arrive in Kathmandu. Upon arrival, meeting assistance and transfer to your hotel. Rest of the day is free. Overnight at Hotel
2 ARRIVE AT KATHMANDU After breakfast commence on a full-day sightseeing tour of Kathmandu valley. Morning, visit Old Kathmandu city with its stone paved lanes which includes Kathmandu Durbar Square, Kumari Ghar, Kasthamandap, Tribhuwan Museum & other temples dotted inside Hanuman Dhoka area. After visit, continue to Swoyambhunath stupa located at hilltop overlooking Kathmandu valley. Swoyambhunath stupa is excellent place to see the cultural harmony among Buddhist & Hindu religion followers in Nepal. Afternoon, visit Pashupatinath temple which is holiest shiva temple for Hindus around the world. Also visit Cementation Ghat located on the bank of Bagmati River. After visit, continue to Boudhnath stupa which is among one of the biggest Buddhist stupa in the world. Boudhnath temple is also known as "Mini Tibet" due to the Tibetan settlement and the Rimpoches reside close to the stupa. Overnight at Hotel.
3 DRIVE KATHMANDU / POKHARA Morning, drive to Pokhara. Pokhara is famous for wonderful views of Himalayas and its snow fed lakes. The reflection of Mt. Fishtail & Annapurna range on Lake Phewa is breathtaking. Pokhara is also base camp for the trekking on Annapurna region with other day hike tours around valley rim. Overnight at Hotel
4 EXCURSION TO SARAGNKOT & POKHARA CITY Early morning, excursion to Sarangkot to see sunrise over Mt. Annapurna, Dulagiri massif & Mt. Fishtail. Sarangkot is also popular as a take-up point for Adventure activities such as Paragliding and Incredible Zipline. Return to hotel for breakfast. Afternoon, commence on a half-day Pokhara city tour. Visit famous Bindavashini temple popular among Hindus, Seti Gorge, Mysterious Devis fall & Gupteshowr Mahadev cave. Overnight at Hotel
5 DRIVE BACK TO KATHMANDU After breakfast drive back to Kathmandu. Those interested can also visit Manakamana temple "Temple of wish full-filling Goddess" by Cable car. Upon arrival, check-in at hotel. Rest of the day is free Overnight at Hotel
6 DEPARTURE TRANSFER Day is free until we provide the transfer to Kathmandu airport in time to connect outbound flight.

Leh

Rajasthan is great for its palaces, tiger reserves, Jain culture, chilies, weaving and marbles. The state is also famous for its camels and sand dunes.

-6 Days-

Day Place Activity
1 Arrival Leh Upon arrival at Leh Airport you will met by Discover Ladakh Adventure representative will greet, assist and transfer you to Hotel in Leh, rest of the day at leisure to acclimatize the high altitude of Ladakh. In the evening take a walk to the local bazaar in Leh and visit Shanti Stupa & Leh Palace. Overnight at hotel in Leh.
2 Excursion to Sham Valley Morning after breakfast drive to Alchi. Enroute visit Magnetic Hill, Gurudwara Patthar Sahib, Confluence of Zanskar and Indus River. Visit Alchi Monastery and drive back to Leh. Just before reaching Leh visit Ladakh Hall of fame. Arrive hotel in Leh for dinner and overnight.
3 Leh - Pangong Lake After Breakfast Leave to Pangong Lake (approx. 145 kms, located on the Indo China Border) through Changla pass 5486 Mtrs. After full day of sightseeing around the Lake check-in at fixed tented camp near the lake and overnight at homestay.
4 Pangong Lake - Leh Leave Pangong Lake after breakfast and retrace the road to Leh across the Changla Pass. Upon reaching Karu, drive across the Indus and visit Hemis Monastery, the biggest and most famous monastery of Ladakh, Later drive on to Leh, enroute visiting the picturesque Thiksey monastery. Dinner and overnight at Hotel.
5 Airport Departure Leave on time for Leh airport to board your flight to Delhi.
